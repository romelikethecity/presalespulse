Best Resources for Pre-Sales & Solutions Engineers in 2026

Solutions engineering sits at the intersection of technical credibility and sales execution. You run discovery calls, build demos, manage POCs, and act as the technical authority in every deal. The resources that matter for this role are scattered across niche communities, vendor blogs, and practitioner newsletters.

We curated this list by talking to SEs at every stage. Every resource here earned its spot by being something practitioners actually recommend to peers.

Newsletters

1. PreSales Collective Newsletter
Free newsletter from the 50,000+ member presales community covering discovery, demos, and deal strategies.

Blogs & Websites

2. PreSales Collective Blog
The presales and solutions engineering blog covering career development, demo skills, and industry trends.
3. We The Sales Engineers
One-stop resource for sales engineering content by Ramzi Marjaba. Blog, coaching, and community.
4. NAASE Blog
Blog from the North American Association of Sales Engineers with industry insights and career opportunities.
5. Mastering Technical Sales
John Care's platform with training curriculum, articles, and resources. 45,000+ students trained.
6. Great Demo!
Peter Cohan's methodology site for creating and executing compelling software demonstrations.
7. PreSales Pulse OUR PICK
Independent career intelligence hub for solutions engineers. Salary data, tool reviews, and job market analysis.

Communities

8. PreSales Collective Community
50,000+ member community with Slack access, peer mentoring, templates, and frameworks.
9. NAASE
Professional association with networking forums, monthly newsletter, and Certified Sales Engineer (CSE) designation.

Tools Worth Knowing

10. Consensus
Demo automation platform for personalized video and click-through experiences by buyer role.
11. Navattic
No-code interactive demo platform for embedding self-guided product tours on websites.
12. Walnut
Demo platform for high-level demo customization and templated live sales demos for ABM campaigns.
13. Demostack
Realistic, repeatable sandbox environments for SE teams needing customizable live demo data.

Podcasts

14. PreSales Podcast (by PSC)
Official podcast from the largest presales community featuring expert-led sessions.
15. We The Sales Engineers Podcast
Ramzi Marjaba interviews seasoned sales engineers. Described as a 'force multiplier' for the profession.

Courses & Training

16. Presales Academy (by PSC)
Self-paced program covering discovery, demo delivery, objection handling, and storytelling. Earns a certificate.
17. Demo2Win (by 2Win! Global)
Annual training program modernizing demo skills in three phases: leadership prep, workshop, and reinforcement.
18. Mastering Technical Sales Training
John Care's professional skills curriculum covering Fundamental through Leadership skills. 45,000+ alumni.
How We Curated This List

Every resource on this page was vetted by talking to practitioners, testing tools firsthand, and reading every newsletter for at least a month. We accept no paid placements. Items marked "OUR PICK" are properties we operate and disclose as such. This list is updated quarterly.
